Grammar:

We use to describe things in numbers!

Positive (+)

S V O
Theres is a cup on the table
There are two cups on the table

Negative (-)

S V O
There is NOT a cup on the table
There are NOT two cups on the table

Question (?)

V S O
Is there a cup in ther table?
Are there two cups on the table?
